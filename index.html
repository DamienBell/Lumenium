Revolutionary Power

Lumenium is a privately held company developing advanced internal combustion ("IC") engine technology. The company’s proprietary, and multi-patented, designs are derived with advanced mathematics and complex geometry but based on elemental shapes, and the simple interaction of concave and convex parts. This combination forms an efficient and compact alternative to piston engines. Lumenium’s core technology escapes the size, weight and cost mandates of traditional IC engines. Lumenium has developed its technology over the course of ten years, creating and testing many generations of running engine models. Lumenium’s numerous patents have issued both domestically and worldwide.

Lumenium designs, fabricates and tests engines at its facilities in Fredericksburg, Virginia.
